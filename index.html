            One point that you'll need to keep in mind is that many sites make use of Javascript to provide behaviors on the page. You might be surprised by this fact when you are looking at a "simple" site, and will learn that you can't truly replicate that site with just HTML and CSS.


            Anyway, I'd suggest that you just poke around the internet and find sites that you find appealing. Don't worry whether they are simple or not or whether they use Javascript or not.
